# What

StarLink is a satellite internet constellation project.

# Why

To provide high-speed internet access to underserved areas.

# How

By deploying thousands of satellites in low Earth orbit.

# When

The project began in 2015 and continues to expand.

# Where

The satellites are launched from various spaceports around the world.

The source code? It's part of the public domain resources.
